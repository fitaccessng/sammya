ℹ️ What is Bank Reconciliation?

Bank reconciliation is the process of comparing your bank account's ledger balance with your actual bank statement to ensure they match.

Common reasons for differences:

  • • Pending deposits not yet processed
  • • Outstanding checks
  • • Bank fees and charges
  • • Timing differences in processing
  • • Interest earned
